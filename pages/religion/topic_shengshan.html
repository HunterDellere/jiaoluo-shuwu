shèng shān Sacred Mountains

China's geography of pilgrimage: the mountains where heaven met earth.

待完成 dài wánchéng · Stub

This entry is in the authoring queue. Two overlapping religious geographies: the Daoist Five Great Mountains (五岳 wǔyuè): Tai Shan in the east, Hua Shan in the west, Heng Shan in the south, Heng Shan in the north (different character), and Song Shan in the center; and the Four Sacred Mountains of Chinese Buddhism (四大名山 sìdàmíngshān): Wutai Shan, Putuo Shan, Emei Shan, and Jiuhua Shan, each associated with a specific bodhisattva.
