    <span class="section-anchor" id="etymology"></span>
    <div class="section-head"><span class="sh-cn">字源</span><span class="sh-py">zìyuán</span><span class="sh-en">Etymology — The Cliff-Shelter</span><span class="sh-rule"></span></div>

    <div class="scholar" data-glyph="廣">
      <div class="scholar-label">字源洞见 zìyuán dòngjiàn · Etymological Insight</div>
      <p>The oracle-bone and bronze-script form of <strong>广</strong> depicts a simple lean-to shelter: a slanted roof line hanging off a cliff face, open on the sides — not a closed room but a broad overhang. The core image is space that extends horizontally outward from a fixed point, sheltered but unenclosed. Wideness, not depth.</p>
      <p>The traditional form <strong>廣</strong> elaborates this: inside the 广 shelter sits <strong>黄 huáng</strong> (yellow/brightness) as a phonetic hint, plus additional strokes suggesting the interior expanse. The simplified character strips all of that back to three strokes — the original cliff-overhang in its purest form.</p>
      <p>From "wide open shelter" the meaning generalized to width, broadness, and extension in all directions — then, in the modern era, to broadcasting: 广播 (guǎngbō), literally "wide casting," the Chinese word for radio and television transmission.</p>
    </div>

    <span class="section-anchor" id="radical"></span>
    <div class="section-head"><span class="sh-cn">部首作用</span><span class="sh-py">bùshǒu zuòyòng</span><span class="sh-en">广 as a Radical — Buildings and Open Spaces</span><span class="sh-rule"></span></div>

    <div class="scholar" data-glyph="广">
      <div class="scholar-label">部首家族 bùshǒu jiāzú · Radical Family</div>
      <p>广 (radical 53) appears in characters that involve structures, rooms, and broad covered spaces:</p>
      <p><strong>店 diàn</strong> (shop, inn) — a place of shelter where goods are kept. <strong>床 chuáng</strong> (bed) — a piece of furniture within a shelter. <strong>厅 tīng</strong> (hall, living room) — an open interior space. <strong>座 zuò</strong> (seat, measure word for buildings) — a seated position within a structure. <strong>庙 miào</strong> (temple) — a sacred shelter. <strong>府 fǔ</strong> (prefecture, official residence) — the broad hall of authority.</p>
      <p>The semantic thread is consistent: where you see 广 at the top-left of a character, look for a built space or something that happens under shelter.</p>
    </div>

    <span class="section-anchor" id="compounds"></span>
    <div class="section-head"><span class="sh-cn">核心词组</span><span class="sh-py">héxīn cízǔ</span><span class="sh-en">Key Compounds</span><span class="sh-rule"></span></div>

    <div class="cards">

      <div class="card c-teal">
        <div class="card-hanzi">广播</div>
        <div class="card-pinyin">guǎngbō</div>
        <div class="card-en">radio broadcast · to broadcast</div>
        <span class="tag-vn">v/n</span>
        <div class="card-note">广 (wide) + 播 (to sow, scatter). The image of scattering signal broadly — the same metaphor as "broadcast" in English.</div>
      </div>

      <div class="card c-teal">
        <div class="card-hanzi">广场</div>
        <div class="card-pinyin">guǎngchǎng</div>
        <div class="card-en">public square · plaza</div>
        <span class="tag-n">n</span>
        <div class="card-note">广 (wide) + 场 (open space). 天安门广场 is the archetypal 广场. Any open civic gathering place.</div>
      </div>

      <div class="card c-teal">
        <div class="card-hanzi">广告</div>
        <div class="card-pinyin">guǎnggào</div>
        <div class="card-en">advertisement · ad</div>
        <span class="tag-n">n</span>
        <div class="card-note">广 (wide) + 告 (to announce). "Wide announcement." The Chinese word for advertising captures the medium better than the English one.</div>
      </div>

      <div class="card c-ochre">
        <div class="card-hanzi">广东</div>
        <div class="card-pinyin">Guǎngdōng</div>
        <div class="card-en">Guangdong Province</div>
        <span class="tag-n">proper n</span>
        <div class="card-note">The "broad east." Paired with 广西 (broad west). Together they defined the southern administrative region of the empire.</div>
      </div>

      <div class="card c-ochre">
        <div class="card-hanzi">广州</div>
        <div class="card-pinyin">Guǎngzhōu</div>
        <div class="card-en">Guangzhou (Canton)</div>
        <span class="tag-n">proper n</span>
        <div class="card-note">Capital of Guangdong. 州 is an administrative region. The historical gateway for maritime trade with the West.</div>
      </div>

      <div class="card c-red">
        <div class="card-hanzi">广泛</div>
        <div class="card-pinyin">guǎngfàn</div>
        <div class="card-en">extensive · widespread · broad</div>
        <span class="tag-adj">adj</span>
        <div class="card-note">广 (wide) + 泛 (to float, spread broadly). Describes scope: 广泛的影响 (widespread influence), 广泛的兴趣 (wide-ranging interests).</div>
      </div>

      <div class="card c-red">
        <div class="card-hanzi">宽广</div>
        <div class="card-pinyin">kuānguǎng</div>
        <div class="card-en">broad · vast · expansive</div>
        <span class="tag-adj">adj</span>
        <div class="card-note">宽 (width from side to side) + 广 (broad expanse). Describes physical space or a generous character: 胸怀宽广 (broad-minded, generous of heart).</div>
      </div>

      <div class="card c-violet">
        <div class="card-hanzi">推广</div>
        <div class="card-pinyin">tuīguǎng</div>
        <div class="card-en">to promote · to spread · to popularize</div>
        <span class="tag-v">v</span>
        <div class="card-note">推 (to push) + 广 (wide). To push something out broadly — used for promoting products, policies, or languages.</div>
      </div>
