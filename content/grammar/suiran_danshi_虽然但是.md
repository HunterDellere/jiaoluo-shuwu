      <span class="topic-hero-eyebrow">Grammar · 语法 yǔfǎ · Concession 让步连词</span>
      <h1 class="topic-hero-title">虽然…但是</h1>
      <span class="topic-hero-title-py">suīrán … dànshì</span>
      <p class="topic-hero-desc">The concession frame: "Although X is true, nevertheless Y." Grants a premise, then pivots to the point that matters — one of the most important logical connectives in Mandarin.</p>
    </header>

    <!-- ── ETYMOLOGY ── -->
    <span class="section-anchor" id="etymology"></span>
    <div class="section-head">
      <span class="sh-cn">字源</span>
      <span class="sh-py">zìyuán</span>
      <span class="sh-en">Etymology — 虽 and 但 Unpacked</span>
      <span class="sh-rule"></span>
    </div>

    <div class="scholar" data-glyph="虽">
      <div class="scholar-label">字源洞见 zìyuán dòngjiàn · Etymological Insight</div>
      <p><strong>虽 suī</strong> is an ancient concessive particle. Its original form showed a type of insect — an animal that buzzes and distracts, something small that nonetheless demands attention. In classical Chinese, 虽 alone carried the full meaning: "even though; even if." It pairs with 然 rán (thus; so) to form 虽然 suīrán — "even though it is thus." 然 is a pointing word: <em>that being so, acknowledging this to be so.</em></p>
      <p><strong>但 dàn</strong> originally meant "only; merely; just." Its classical sense was restrictive. In modern Mandarin, it pivots to mean "but; however; yet" — the word that changes direction. Paired with 是 shì as 但是, it becomes the standard formal "but."</p>
      <p>The full construction 虽然…但是 is a complete logical unit: acknowledge the conceded premise (虽然), then assert the overriding conclusion (但是). The rhetorical power is in the pivot — Chinese discourse typically grants what cannot be denied before making its real point.</p>
    </div>

    <!-- ── STRUCTURE ── -->
    <span class="section-anchor" id="structure"></span>
    <div class="section-head">
      <span class="sh-cn">句型框架</span>
      <span class="sh-py">jùxíng kuàngjià</span>
      <span class="sh-en">Structure — The Concession Frame</span>
      <span class="sh-rule"></span>
    </div>

    <div class="pattern">
      <span class="label">基本框架 jīběn kuàngjià · Basic Frame</span>
      <strong>虽然 [conceded premise A]，但是 [real point B]</strong><br>
      "Although A is true, B is what actually matters."<br><br>
      <strong>虽然…但</strong> — shortened (spoken/written)<br>
      <strong>虽然…可是</strong> — softer pivot (可是 kěshì = but/however, slightly gentler)<br>
      <strong>虽然…然而</strong> — formal/literary (然而 rán'ér = nevertheless)
    </div>

    <div class="note">
      <span class="note-label">语法 yǔfǎ · Subject Position</span>
      When both clauses share a subject: the subject comes <em>before</em> 虽然, not after: <strong>他虽然很忙，但是还是来了</strong> (He, although very busy, still came). When the clauses have different subjects, each subject follows its conjunction: 虽然天气很冷，但是大家都出来了 (Although the weather was cold, everyone came out anyway).
    </div>

    <!-- ── EXAMPLES ── -->
    <span class="section-anchor" id="examples"></span>
    <div class="section-head">
      <span class="sh-cn">例句</span>

    <div class="cards">

      <div class="card c-vn">
        <div class="card-head">
          <span class="card-cn">虽然…但是 (basic)</span>
          <span class="card-py">suīrán … dànshì</span>
          <span class="card-en">although … but</span>
        </div>
        <div class="tags"><span class="tag tag-v">Conj 连词 liáncí</span></div>
        <div class="card-def">
          The foundational concession pattern. 虽然 grants something true; 但是 asserts what is more important or what overrides it. The pivot is the rhetorical heart of Chinese argumentation.
        </div>
        <div class="examples">
          <div class="example">
            <div class="ex-cn">虽然他很努力，但是结果还是不理想。</div>
            <div class="ex-py">Suīrán tā hěn nǔlì, dànshì jiéguǒ hái shì bù lǐxiǎng.</div>
            <div class="ex-en">Although he worked very hard, the result was still not ideal.</div>
          </div>
          <div class="example">
            <div class="ex-cn">这件衣服虽然贵，但是质量很好。</div>
            <div class="ex-py">Zhè jiàn yīfu suīrán guì, dànshì zhìliàng hěn hǎo.</div>
            <div class="ex-en">Although this piece of clothing is expensive, the quality is excellent.</div>
          </div>
          <div class="example">
            <div class="ex-cn">虽然我们分开了，但是我永远记得你。</div>
            <div class="ex-py">Suīrán wǒmen fēnkāi le, dànshì wǒ yǒngyuǎn jìde nǐ.</div>
            <div class="ex-en">Although we've parted, I will always remember you.</div>
          </div>
          <div class="example">
            <div class="ex-cn">他虽然说得好，但是做得不如说的。</div>
            <div class="ex-py">Tā suīrán shuō de hǎo, dànshì zuò de bùrú shuō de.</div>
            <div class="ex-en">Although he speaks well, his actions don't match his words.</div>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>

    <div class="cards">

      <div class="card c-v">
        <div class="card-head">
          <span class="card-cn">尽管…还是/仍然</span>
          <span class="card-py">jǐnguǎn … háishì / réngrán</span>
          <span class="card-en">despite … still; even so … still</span>
        </div>
        <div class="tags"><span class="tag tag-v">Conj 连词 liáncí</span></div>
        <div class="card-def">
          尽管 jǐnguǎn (despite; even though) + 还是/仍然 (still/nevertheless). Similar to 虽然…但是 but with a stronger sense of "despite all that" — the conceded circumstance is more emphatic, the result more surprising.
        </div>
        <div class="examples">
          <div class="example">
            <div class="ex-cn">尽管下着大雨，她还是按时来了。</div>
            <div class="ex-py">Jǐnguǎn xià zhe dà yǔ, tā háishì ànshí lái le.</div>
            <div class="ex-en">Despite the heavy rain, she still arrived on time.</div>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="card c-v">
        <div class="card-head">
          <span class="card-cn">即使…也</span>
          <span class="card-py">jíshǐ … yě</span>
          <span class="card-en">even if … still; even were it the case that</span>
        </div>
        <div class="tags"><span class="tag tag-v">Conj 连词 liáncí</span></div>
        <div class="card-def">
          即使 jíshǐ (even if; even supposing) + 也 yě (also; still). A hypothetical concession — "even if X were true, Y would still follow." Unlike 虽然 (which grants something actually true), 即使 can refer to a hypothetical or extreme case.
        </div>
        <div class="examples">
          <div class="example">
            <div class="ex-cn">即使很难，我也不会放弃。</div>
            <div class="ex-py">Jíshǐ hěn nán, wǒ yě bù huì fàngqì.</div>
            <div class="ex-en">Even if it is very difficult, I still won't give up.</div>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
