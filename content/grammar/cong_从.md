    <header class="topic-hero">
      <span class="topic-hero-eyebrow">Grammar · 语法 yǔfǎ</span>
      <h1 class="topic-hero-title">从</h1>
      <span class="topic-hero-title-py">cóng</span>
      <p class="topic-hero-desc">From, since, following — 从 marks the starting point of motion in space (from a place), origin in time (since a moment), and adherence to a course (following an instruction or rule). It is the counterpart to 到 dào (to, arriving at).</p>
    </header>

    <span class="section-anchor" id="overview"></span>
    <div class="section-head">
      <span class="sh-cn">概览</span>
      <span class="sh-py">gàilǎn</span>
      <span class="sh-en">从 — Origin and Starting Points</span>
      <span class="sh-rule"></span>
    </div>

    <div class="scholar" data-glyph="从">
      <div class="scholar-label">语法洞见 yǔfǎ dòngjiàn · Grammar Insight</div>
      <p><strong>从 cóng</strong> is a preposition that marks the starting point — in space, in time, or in a sequence. Its structural position: 从 comes before the noun that marks the origin, and is followed by the main verb phrase. The preposition phrase acts as an adverb modifying the main verb.</p>
      <p><strong>Spatial origin:</strong> 从北京来 (come from Beijing), 从这里到那里 (from here to there), 从左边进 (enter from the left). The endpoint is usually marked by 到 dào: <strong>从 + [origin] + 到 + [destination]</strong> is the standard frame for expressing routes and journeys.</p>
      <p><strong>Temporal origin:</strong> 从小时候 (since childhood), 从上个月开始 (starting from last month), 从明天起 (starting from tomorrow). The endpoint marker is again 到: 从早到晚 (from morning to night), 从古到今 (from ancient times to now).</p>
      <p><strong>Following/compliance sense:</strong> 从命 (to follow orders), 服从 (to obey), 从事 (to engage in, to follow an occupation). This sense is more classical/formal but appears in modern vocabulary.</p>
    </div>

    <span class="section-anchor" id="spatial"></span>
    <div class="section-head">
      <span class="sh-cn">空间</span>
      <span class="sh-py">kōngjiān</span>
      <span class="sh-en">Spatial Origin — From a Place</span>
      <span class="sh-rule"></span>
    </div>

    <div class="pattern">
      <span class="label">空间句型 kōngjiān jùxíng · Spatial Pattern</span>
      <strong>从 + [Place] + [Verb]</strong><br>
      从北京来。Came from Beijing.<br>
      从这里走。Go from here.<br>
      从窗户进来。Come in through the window.<br><br>
      <strong>从 + [Origin] + 到 + [Destination] + [Verb]</strong><br>
      从上海到广州坐高铁要几小时？How many hours by high-speed rail from Shanghai to Guangzhou?<br>
      从家到公司，我每天走路。I walk from home to the office every day.
    </div>

    <div class="cards">

      <div class="card c-v">
        <div class="card-head">
          <span class="card-cn">从…到… (spatial)</span>
          <span class="card-py">cóng … dào …</span>
          <span class="card-en">from [place] to [place] — marking a route or journey</span>
        </div>
        <div class="tags"><span class="tag tag-v">Structure 句型 jùxíng</span></div>
        <div class="card-def">
          The core spatial frame: 从 marks the origin, 到 marks the destination, the main verb follows (or is implied). Word order: <strong>Subject + 从 + Origin + 到 + Destination + Verb + Duration/manner</strong>. 从这儿到机场要多久？= How long from here to the airport? 从南到北 = from south to north. Note: 从 + place typically describes the literal point of departure, while 离 lí + place describes the distance gap between two static locations (这里离火车站很近 = the train station is close from here).
        </div>
        <div class="examples">
          <div class="example">
            <div class="ex-cn">从北京到上海，高铁只需要四个多小时。</div>
            <div class="ex-py">Cóng Běijīng dào Shànghǎi, gāotiě zhǐ xūyào sì gè duō xiǎoshí.</div>
            <div class="ex-en">From Beijing to Shanghai, the high-speed train only takes a little over four hours.</div>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>

    <span class="section-anchor" id="temporal"></span>
    <div class="section-head">
      <span class="sh-cn">时间</span>
      <span class="sh-py">shíjiān</span>
      <span class="sh-en">Temporal Origin — Since a Time</span>
      <span class="sh-rule"></span>
    </div>

    <div class="pattern">
      <span class="label">时间句型 shíjiān jùxíng · Temporal Pattern</span>
      <strong>从 + [Time] + 起 / 开始 + [Verb Phrase]</strong><br>
      从明天开始，我要早起。Starting from tomorrow, I'm going to get up early.<br>
      从那以后，他就没再说话了。After that (from that point on), he never spoke again.<br>
      从小到大，她一直很努力。From childhood to adulthood, she has always worked hard.<br><br>
      <strong>从 + [Time] + 到 + [Time]</strong><br>
      从早上八点到下午五点 = from 8 AM to 5 PM
    </div>

    <div class="cards">

      <div class="card c-v">
        <div class="card-head">
          <span class="card-cn">从…到… (temporal)</span>
          <span class="card-py">cóng … dào …</span>
          <span class="card-en">from [time] to [time] — marking a duration or span</span>
        </div>
        <div class="tags"><span class="tag tag-v">Structure 句型 jùxíng</span></div>
        <div class="card-def">
          The temporal frame exactly parallels the spatial one: 从 marks the start of a time period, 到 marks the end. 从小到大 (from small to big — from childhood to adulthood — one of the most common fixed phrases). 从古到今 (from ancient times to today). 从头到尾 (from beginning to end — lit. "from head to tail"). The 从…到… frame can be metaphorical: 从理论到实践 (from theory to practice).
        </div>
        <div class="examples">
          <div class="example">
            <div class="ex-cn">从小到大，父母从来没有对我说过不好听的话。</div>
            <div class="ex-py">Cóng xiǎo dào dà, fùmǔ cónglái méiyǒu duì wǒ shuō guò bù hǎotīng de huà.</div>
            <div class="ex-en">From childhood to adulthood, my parents never said unkind words to me.</div>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>

    </div>

    <div class="cards">

      <div class="card c-v">
        <div class="card-head">
          <span class="card-cn">从来</span>
          <span class="card-py">cónglái</span>
          <span class="card-en">always; never — used with negation for absolute denial</span>
        </div>
        <div class="tags"><span class="tag tag-v">Adverb 副词 fùcí</span></div>
        <div class="card-def">
          从 cóng (from) + 来 lái (come — here as "up to now"). 从来 without negation = "always, consistently" (formal/literary). In practice, 从来 almost always appears with negation: 从来不 (never — habitual negation) or 从来没有 (have never — experiential negation). 从来不 describes habitual non-behavior: 我从来不喝酒 "I never drink." 从来没有 describes an experience that has never occurred: 我从来没有去过日本 "I've never been to Japan." The difference between 从来不 and 从来没有 mirrors 不 vs. 没有 generally.
        </div>
        <div class="examples">
          <div class="example">
            <div class="ex-cn">他从来不迟到，今天居然来晚了。</div>
            <div class="ex-py">Tā cónglái bù chídào, jīntiān jūrán lái wǎn le.</div>
            <div class="ex-en">He never arrives late — it's surprising he was late today.</div>
          </div>
          <div class="example">
            <div class="ex-cn">我从来没有见过下这么大的雪。</div>
            <div class="ex-py">Wǒ cónglái méiyǒu jiàn guò xià zhème dà de xuě.</div>
            <div class="ex-en">I've never seen snow fall this heavily before.</div>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>

      <div class="card c-v">
        <div class="card-head">
          <span class="card-cn">从此</span>
          <span class="card-py">cóngcǐ</span>
          <span class="card-en">from then on; henceforth — narrative turning point</span>
        </div>
        <div class="tags"><span class="tag tag-v">Adverb 副词 fùcí</span></div>
        <div class="card-def">
          从 cóng (from) + 此 cǐ (this; here). "From this point on" — a narrative marker that signals a permanent change of state after a critical event. Common in stories, histories, and personal narratives: 从此他再也没有回来 "from then on, he never returned." 从此以后 is the fuller form (from this point forward). Often appears in fairy tale endings: 他们从此过上了幸福的生活 "they lived happily ever after" (lit. "from then on they lived a happy life").
        </div>
        <div class="examples">
          <div class="example">
            <div class="ex-cn">经历了那次失败之后，他从此更加努力学习。</div>
            <div class="ex-py">Jīnglì le nà cì shībài zhīhòu, tā cóngcǐ gèngjiā nǔlì xuéxí.</div>
            <div class="ex-en">After that failure, he studied even harder from then on.</div>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
