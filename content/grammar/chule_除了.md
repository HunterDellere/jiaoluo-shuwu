    <header class="topic-hero">
      <span class="topic-hero-eyebrow">Grammar · 语法 yǔfǎ</span>
      <h1 class="topic-hero-title">除了</h1>
      <span class="topic-hero-title-py">chú le</span>
      <p class="topic-hero-desc">Except, besides, in addition to — 除了 is the particle that either carves an exception out of a universal statement or adds one more item to a set. Which meaning it carries depends entirely on the particle that follows: 都 signals exclusion; 还/也 signals inclusion.</p>
    </header>

    <span class="section-anchor" id="overview"></span>
    <div class="section-head">
      <span class="sh-cn">概览</span>
      <span class="sh-py">gàilǎn</span>
      <span class="sh-en">Two Uses — The Critical Distinction</span>
      <span class="sh-rule"></span>
    </div>

    <div class="scholar" data-glyph="除">
      <div class="scholar-label">语法洞见 yǔfǎ dòngjiàn · Grammar Insight</div>
      <p><strong>除了 chúle</strong> = 除 chú (to remove; to exclude; division in math) + 了 le (aspect marker used here as part of the preposition). The character 除 already contains the key meaning: to remove something from a set.</p>
      <p><strong>Pattern 1 — Exclusion (except):</strong> 除了 + [exception] + 以外/之外, [rest of set] + 都 + [Verb Phrase]. The 都 (all) signals that the statement applies to every member of the set <em>except</em> the item named after 除了. 除了他，大家都来了 = "Except for him, everyone came." The 以外/之外 is optional but common in formal speech.</p>
      <p><strong>Pattern 2 — Inclusion (besides/in addition to):</strong> 除了 + [base] + 以外/之外, [Subject] + 还/也 + [Verb Phrase]. The 还 (also; in addition) or 也 (also) signals that something is added to an already existing set. 除了英语，她还会法语 = "Besides English, she also knows French." This is the inverse of Pattern 1: instead of removing an item, you are naming a base and then adding more.</p>
      <p>The two patterns are disambiguated by the correlating adverb: <strong>都 → exclusion / 还·也 → inclusion</strong>. This is the single most important rule for this structure.</p>
    </div>

    <span class="section-anchor" id="except"></span>
    <div class="section-head">
      <span class="sh-cn">排除</span>
      <span class="sh-py">páichú</span>
      <span class="sh-en">Exclusion — Except (除了…都)</span>
      <span class="sh-rule"></span>
    </div>

    <div class="pattern">
      <span class="label">排除句型 páichú jùxíng · Exclusion Pattern</span>
      <strong>除了 + [Exception] (以外/之外), [Subject] + 都 + [VP]</strong><br>
      除了周末，他每天都来上班。Except for weekends, he comes to work every day.<br>
      除了咖啡，我什么都不喝。Except for coffee, I don't drink anything.<br>
      除了她，大家都同意了。Except for her, everyone agreed.<br>
      除了这件，别的衣服都太贵了。Except for this one, all the other clothes are too expensive.
    </div>

    <div class="cards">

      <div class="card c-v">
        <div class="card-head">
          <span class="card-cn">除了…都 (exclusion)</span>
          <span class="card-py">chúle … dōu</span>
          <span class="card-en">except [X], everything/everyone [VP]</span>
        </div>
        <div class="tags"><span class="tag tag-v">Structure 句型 jùxíng</span></div>
        <div class="card-def">
          The exclusion frame: 除了 names the exception, 都 sweeps up the remainder. The structure can also be negated: 除了…，都不… (none except for X). 除了他，我谁都不认识 = "Except for him, I don't know anyone" — note the 都不 (none). Useful contrast: 只有 zhǐyǒu (only) is a related exclusive: 只有他来了 (only he came) = 除了他，别人都没来 (except for him, no one else came).
        </div>
        <div class="examples">
          <div class="example">
            <div class="ex-cn">除了下雨，他每天都去跑步。</div>
            <div class="ex-py">Chúle xià yǔ, tā měitiān dōu qù pǎobù.</div>
            <div class="ex-en">Except when it rains, he goes running every day.</div>
          </div>
          <div class="example">
            <div class="ex-cn">除了这道题，其他题我都做完了。</div>
            <div class="ex-py">Chúle zhè dào tí, qítā tí wǒ dōu zuò wán le.</div>
            <div class="ex-en">Except for this problem, I've finished all the other questions.</div>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>

    </div>

    <span class="section-anchor" id="besides"></span>
    <div class="section-head">
      <span class="sh-cn">包括</span>
      <span class="sh-py">bāokuò</span>
      <span class="sh-en">Inclusion — Besides / In Addition To (除了…还/也)</span>
      <span class="sh-rule"></span>
    </div>

    <div class="pattern">
      <span class="label">包括句型 bāokuò jùxíng · Inclusion Pattern</span>
      <strong>除了 + [Base] (以外/之外), [Subject] + 还/也 + [VP]</strong><br>
      除了中文，她还会英文和法文。Besides Chinese, she also knows English and French.<br>
      除了工作，我还有很多爱好。Besides work, I also have many hobbies.<br>
      除了买书，他们还参观了博物馆。Besides buying books, they also visited a museum.
    </div>

    <div class="cards">

      <div class="card c-v">
        <div class="card-head">
          <span class="card-cn">除了…还/也 (inclusion)</span>
          <span class="card-py">chúle … hái / yě</span>
          <span class="card-en">besides [X], [Subject] also [VP]</span>
        </div>
        <div class="tags"><span class="tag tag-v">Structure 句型 jùxíng</span></div>
        <div class="card-def">
          The inclusion frame: 除了 names the base that is already assumed or established; 还 or 也 signals that the new information adds to that base rather than replacing it. 还 (in addition, moreover) is slightly more common than 也 (also) in this construction. 此外 cǐwài (besides this; in addition — more formal) and 另外 lìngwài (in addition; separately) are near-synonyms in formal writing. 不但…而且 bùdàn…érqiě (not only…but also) is a related additive structure with stronger emphasis.
        </div>
        <div class="examples">
          <div class="example">
            <div class="ex-cn">除了会弹钢琴，她还会拉小提琴。</div>
            <div class="ex-py">Chúle huì tán gāngqín, tā hái huì lā xiǎotíqín.</div>
            <div class="ex-en">Besides being able to play piano, she can also play violin.</div>
          </div>
          <div class="example">
            <div class="ex-cn">除了价格贵，这家餐厅其实很不错。</div>
            <div class="ex-py">Chúle jiàgé guì, zhè jiā cāntīng qíshí hěn bùcuò.</div>
            <div class="ex-en">Besides being expensive, this restaurant is actually quite good.</div>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
