入门 rùmén

Quick Start

A short tour of the nook — language, philosophy, culture, food. Read in order, or wander.

    Every character is a door.

    Shūwū — 角落書屋, the corner — is a quiet personal nook for slow notes on the Chinese language and the civilisation that gave rise to it. Kept here in the open so other curious readers can pull up a chair and follow a thread for an afternoon.

    Each entry is written to be lingered over, not drilled. Sources include Outlier Linguistic Solutions, Wenlin, Pleco, the Hanyu Da Cidian, and classical primary texts where applicable.
