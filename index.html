Quick Start

New here? Start with these. Language, philosophy, culture, food — a sampler that shows what the nook is for.

    Every character is a door.

    Shūwū — 角落書屋, the corner bookroom — is a personal project: one reader's attempt to understand Chinese not just as a language to be memorised, but as a civilisation to be explored. Everything here is written slowly and for keeping.

    Entries draw on Outlier Linguistic Solutions, Wenlin, Pleco, the Hanyu Da Cidian, and primary classical texts. If you find something wrong, the corrections link below is there for a reason.
