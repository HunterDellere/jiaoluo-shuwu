    说明 shuōmíng

    How to read

    Page types
    字Character. One glyph, with its etymology, decomposition, and compounds.
    词Vocabulary. A word or concept that carries cultural weight.
    法Grammar. A particle, structure, or grammatical pattern.
    题Topic. A short essay on a cultural, historical, or philosophical subject.
    Anatomy of an entry
    ①Hero. Glyph, pinyin, gloss, and HSK or radical chips.
    ②Etymology. How the character came to look and mean what it does.
    ③Compounds and chengyu. The character as a building block.
    ④Retention hook. A memory image so the glyph sticks.
    Navigation
    ⌕Press / anywhere to jump to search.
    ↓Use ↑ ↓ to step through results, Enter to open.
    ⌘Each entry has a sticky table of contents on the side.
    ⊞Section titles in the index are clickable to collapse.
